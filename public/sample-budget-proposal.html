Budget Approval Request for Fiscal Year 2025

Executive Summary

This document presents the comprehensive budget proposal for KEDCO's fiscal year 2025 operations. The proposed budget totals NGN 2,450,000,000 and represents a strategic allocation of resources to support our core operations, infrastructure development, and service delivery improvements.

Background

In line with KEDCO's strategic objectives for 2025, this budget proposal has been developed after thorough analysis of operational requirements, infrastructure needs, and regulatory obligations. The allocation prioritizes critical areas including network expansion, technical upgrades, customer service enhancement, and operational efficiency.

Key Highlights

Infrastructure Development: The allocation of NGN 650M for infrastructure represents our commitment to expanding service coverage and improving network reliability across our service areas.

Operational Excellence: The operations and maintenance budget ensures continuity of services and addresses critical maintenance needs identified in Q4 2024 assessments.

Human Capital: Investment in human resources includes competitive compensation packages and comprehensive training programs to build technical capacity.

Justification

This budget reflects a 12% increase from FY 2024, primarily driven by:

  • Expanded service coverage requirements
  • Increased infrastructure maintenance needs
  • Regulatory compliance obligations
  • Technology modernization initiatives
  • Enhanced customer service standards

Recommendation

The Finance Department recommends approval of this budget proposal to enable KEDCO to meet its operational obligations, pursue strategic objectives, and deliver improved services to our customers in FY 2025.
