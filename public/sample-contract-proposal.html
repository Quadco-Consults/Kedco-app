Partnership Contract Proposal

Network Infrastructure Modernization Project

1. Executive Summary

XYZ Engineering & Solutions Ltd. is pleased to submit this comprehensive proposal for partnership with KEDCO on the Network Infrastructure Modernization Project. Our proposal encompasses the supply, installation, and commissioning of advanced metering infrastructure and network equipment to enhance KEDCO's service delivery and operational efficiency.

2. Company Profile

XYZ Engineering & Solutions Ltd. is a leading provider of electrical infrastructure solutions in Nigeria with over 15 years of experience in the power sector. We have successfully completed similar projects for multiple distribution companies across West Africa, delivering cutting-edge solutions that improve operational efficiency and customer satisfaction.

3. Proposed Scope of Work

3.1 Supply of Materials

  • 15,000 units of Smart Prepaid Meters (Single Phase)
  • 5,000 units of Smart Prepaid Meters (Three Phase)
  • 200 Distribution Transformers (500 KVA)
  • 150 km of Low Voltage Cables
  • Advanced Metering Infrastructure Software

3.2 Installation Services

  • Complete meter installation across designated coverage areas
  • Transformer installation and commissioning
  • Network cable laying and termination
  • System integration and testing

3.3 Training & Support

  • Technical training for KEDCO staff (40 personnel)
  • 24-month warranty and maintenance support
  • Remote monitoring system setup
