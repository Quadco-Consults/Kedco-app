Memorandum for Approval

1. Purpose

This memorandum seeks the Managing Director's approval for the proposed budget allocation for Fiscal Year 2025. The Finance Department has conducted a comprehensive review of operational requirements and strategic objectives to formulate this budget proposal.

2. Background

In line with our strategic planning cycle, the Finance Department has prepared the FY 2025 budget based on:

  • Historical expenditure analysis from FY 2024
  • Projected operational requirements for 2025
  • Strategic infrastructure development initiatives
  • Regulatory and compliance obligations
  • Projected revenue streams and cost optimization measures

3. Proposed Budget Summary

Budget Category Description Amount (NGN) % of Total
Operations & Maintenance Day-to-day operational expenses and routine maintenance 850,000,000 34.7%
Infrastructure Development Network expansion, upgrades, and capital projects 650,000,000 26.5%

TOTAL PROPOSED BUDGET 2,450,000,000 100%

4. Justification

The proposed budget of NGN 2.45 billion represents a 12% increase from FY 2024 allocation. This increase is justified by the following factors:

  • Inflation Adjustment: 8% inflation rate adjustment for operational costs
  • Network Expansion: Planned coverage extension to 15 new communities
  • Technology Upgrade: Migration to smart metering infrastructure
  • Regulatory Compliance: Enhanced reporting and compliance requirements
  • Staff Development: Comprehensive training programs for technical capacity building

5. Expected Outcomes

Approval of this budget will enable KEDCO to:

  • Maintain uninterrupted service delivery across all service areas
  • Expand network coverage to underserved communities
  • Improve operational efficiency through technology adoption
  • Enhance customer satisfaction through improved service quality
  • Meet all regulatory and statutory obligations
  • Build technical capacity through staff training and development

ACTION REQUIRED

The Finance Department respectfully requests the Managing Director's approval for the FY 2025 budget as detailed above. Upon approval, the budget will be implemented effective January 1, 2025, with quarterly reviews and adjustments as necessary.

6. Recommendation

The Finance Department strongly recommends approval of this budget to ensure KEDCO's operational continuity, strategic growth, and ability to meet stakeholder expectations in FY 2025.
